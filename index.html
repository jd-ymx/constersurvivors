Survive against relentless waves of monsters in this addictive roguelike adventure game.

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where you face endless waves of increasingly difficult monsters. Choose your character, unlock powerful weapons and abilities, and see how long you can last against the monstrous hordes.

As you progress, you'll collect experience to level up, choose new abilities, and upgrade your arsenal to become more powerful. But beware - the monsters will also grow stronger and more numerous with each passing minute!

With its addictive gameplay loop, diverse character builds, and challenging progression system, Monster Survivors offers endless hours of monster-slaying fun.

Key Features

Endless Action

Face wave after wave of increasingly difficult monsters in an endless battle for survival.

Character Progression

Level up your character, unlock new abilities, and upgrade your weapons to become more powerful.

Diverse Builds

Create unique character builds with different combinations of weapons, abilities, and passive upgrades.

Boss Battles

Test your skills against massive boss monsters with unique abilities and attack patterns.

Unlockable Content

Discover new characters, weapons, and abilities as you progress through the game.

Easy to Learn

Simple controls make the game accessible to everyone, but mastering it takes skill and strategy.

How to Play

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Your character attacks automatically when monsters are in range
  • Collect experience gems to level up
  • Choose new abilities and upgrades when you level up
  • Survive as long as possible against the monster hordes
  • On mobile, use the virtual joystick to control movement
